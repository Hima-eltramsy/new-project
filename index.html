about me:

I am Colonel (Ret.) Tarek Mohamed Samir from Egypt, an innovator and inventor of a groundbreaking technology aimed at enhancing the speed, range, accuracy, and power of small-caliber projectiles for pistols, rifles, and heavy machine guns. This advanced technology represents a revolutionary leap in the ammunition industry, creating a product that is unmatched and highly demanded globally. It has the potential to position the producing country as a global leader in advanced ammunition manufacturing. Due to the lack of resources and funding in Egypt for testing and developing this innovation, I am looking to implement this technology in Saudi Arabia, a country known for its supportive environment for innovation and technological advancements. Collaboration on this project promises tremendous economic returns and strengthens Saudi Arabia's global leadership in advanced ammunition production.
